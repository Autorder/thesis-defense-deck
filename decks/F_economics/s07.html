Interpretation and threshold
7

A resilience expenditure, not an investment

Energy saturation

By 10–15 kWh the marginal benefit of storage is already small in every configuration — more capacity adds little.

Resilience, not return

The battery is a resilience-and-independence expenditure — its own contribution never recovers its own cost, in any configuration.

Bottom line: a static, buy-today payback already shows the battery never recovers its own cost — so discounting only deepens the loss. It matters only if a policy change first flips the simple case positive.
