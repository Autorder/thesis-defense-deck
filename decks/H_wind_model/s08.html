Limitations and design recommendations

Declared limitations

No wake effect; linear scaling in turbine count; no wind cross-validation; fixed turbine spec.

Recommendations

Haifa: 3–4 turbines as a primary lever. Jerusalem: the 1 kW turbine is not worthwhile.

Decade decay

Insensitive to turbine count — decay is driven by solar surplus & battery cycles, not wind.

Bottom line: wind is fixed, additive and set entirely by the local resource — a primary lever in Haifa, negligible in Jerusalem; storage amplifies it.
