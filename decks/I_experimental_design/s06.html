Why this structure

A base grid fixes the design space; each study perturbs one parameter and freezes the rest — so every result is attributable to a single cause.

What is NOT done

No simultaneous full-cross of all parameters — it explodes combinatorially with little added insight.

On the 500

Sum of the four matrices; some points recur (12,000 kWh = base), so unique configurations are fewer.

500 deterministic hourly simulations · 8,760 h/yr × 10 years each · 43.8 M hourly steps.
